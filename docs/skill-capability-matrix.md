## Reality Check and Implementation Status

A significant portion of the referenced stack artifacts describe deployment visions, architectural specifications, governance models, or simulated operational states rather than independently verified production systems. Capabilities should therefore be classified by maturity before being used in commercial, legal, investor, or compliance materials.

Known baseline indicators include:

- Overall platform completion is approximately 46%.
- Stephanie.ai completion is approximately 81%.
- The voice pipeline remains blocked by live gating and backend integration.

This distinction is commercially and legally important because some capabilities appear architecturally defined but not necessarily fully deployed or live.
